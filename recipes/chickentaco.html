Fiesta Slow Cooker Shredded Chicken Taco

This chicken tacos recipe is easy to make in a slow cooker. Spoon the filling into warm tortillas for a very tasty meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 3 tablespoons taco seasoning mix
  • 1 pound skinless, boneless chicken breasts

Directions

  1. Step: Combine chicken broth and taco seasoning mix in a bowl.
  2. Step: Place chicken in a slow cooker. Pour chicken broth mixture over chicken.
  3. Step: Cook on low for 6 to 8 hours. Shred chicken.
